# Pulse

Pulse is a Twitch-style livestream platform built as a vehicle for learning <span className="ds-mark">Kafka</span> and **distributed-systems architecture** deeply. These docs are the tutorial-shaped read of the system: what was built, why, and the concepts each decision teaches. They are organised in three tiers.

**[Concepts →](/concepts)**
The distributed-systems ideas the build forces you to confront — KRaft, healthchecks, auto-topic-creation, named volumes — one page each.

**[Architecture →](/architecture)**
The system topology and the infrastructure substrate. What runs, what talks to what, and what is still on the drawing board.

**[Journey →](/journey/foundations)**
Progress by phase and epic: lessons unlocked, issues closed, and the war stories worth remembering.

<Callout variant="note" title="This is a study artifact">
  These pages are **downstream** of the canonical sources — the Architecture
  Decision Records in `docs/adr/` and the domain glossary in `CONTEXT.md`. When
  this site disagrees with an ADR, the ADR wins and the site is wrong. Nothing

  for why the site exists and why it is deliberately not a source of truth.
